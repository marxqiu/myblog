I am a mathematics educator interested in how students learn, how classrooms can make thinking visible, and how teachers can design better tasks with better tools.

This site collects my writing, reading notes, and classroom resources. The common thread is practical intellectual work: finding clearer explanations, building useful representations, and turning ideas from cognitive science, curriculum design, and AI into things that can actually help a lesson.

## Current Interests

- Inquiry-based mathematics teaching
- Curriculum design and MYP mathematics
- AI-assisted planning, feedback, and resource creation
- Cognitive science, mental models, and learning strategy
