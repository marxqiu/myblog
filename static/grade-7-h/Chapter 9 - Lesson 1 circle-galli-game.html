Circle Galli

A fast-paced matching game for circle vocabulary!

How to Play:

  • Each round, 4 cards will be flipped face-up on the screen.
  • Cards can show a Word, a Definition, or a Diagram.
  • If you spot ANY TWO cards that describe the same concept, race to ring the bell!
  • The teacher will click Reveal Answer. If you are correct, your group gets 1 point.
  • Beware: Sometimes there are NO matches at all!
